About The Artist

Malak: The Artist Behind The Pencil

Malak is a passionate anime artist who specializes in creating stunning monochrome drawings. With over 5 years of experience, she has developed a unique style that combines traditional pencil techniques with modern anime aesthetics.

Her artwork focuses on emotional expression, intricate details, and capturing the essence of characters through careful shading and line work. Each piece is created with dedication, often taking 20-40 hours to complete.

Malak believes that black and white drawings have a unique power to convey emotions without the distraction of color, allowing viewers to focus on the composition, expression, and story within each piece.

She draws inspiration from both classic and contemporary anime, as well as from everyday emotions and experiences that she transforms into captivating visual stories.
